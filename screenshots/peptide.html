What it does
GHK-Cu is a tripeptide naturally found in your blood that binds copper to form a small healing complex. It signals the body to repair tissue, produce collagen, and reduce inflammation. Levels fall with age — roughly 60–70% lower at 60 than at 20 — which researchers think contributes to slower healing.
